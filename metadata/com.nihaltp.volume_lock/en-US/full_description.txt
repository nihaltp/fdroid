Volume Lock gives you fine-grained control over your Android audio behavior with two core features:

- Volume Lock: when the screen turns off, current media, ring, notification, and alarm volumes are locked and automatically restored if changed.
- App Volume Lock: saves and restores media volume for selected apps when you return to them.

Key highlights:
- Tracks and restores audio levels automatically
- Lets you choose which apps participate in App Volume Lock
- Keeps behavior consistent after reboot

Permissions:
- Do Not Disturb / Notification Policy access is needed to lock ring and notification volumes on Android 6+
- Accessibility service is needed to detect foreground app changes for App Volume Lock

Built with Flutter
